Living the eLife

The Challenge

There are over 50,000 pensioners in Jamaica who rely on monthly payments. To maintain these benefits, pensioners must submit a quarterly "Proof of Life" certificate, a manual process requiring in-person verification by a Justice of the Peace (JP) or designated notary.

This creates significant challenges for pensioners in remote areas, those with limited mobility, or individuals struggling to access notarial services. Delays in submission risk interruptions to critical pension payments.

Our Solution

eLife is a secure, optional digital pathway designed to coexist with traditional methods. It empowers pensioners who prefer or require a streamlined, remote solution to verify their life status, while reducing administrative strain on the AGD and designated notaries.

A process that on average took a week can be done in minutes.
