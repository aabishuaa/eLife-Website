About eLife

Transforming the way Jamaican pensioners verify their life status through secure, accessible digital technology.

Our Mission

eLife aims to modernize the traditional "Proof of Life" certification process, ensuring that Jamaica's 50,000 pensioners can easily verify their status without the barriers associated with manual paperwork and in-person verification.

We are committed to empowering pensioners with technology that respects their dignity, preserves their independence, and ensures timely delivery of their essential benefits.

The Challenge We're Solving

Mobility Issues

Many pensioners face challenges traveling to meet with a Justice of the Peace or notary, especially those with physical limitations or those living in remote areas.

Time-Consuming Process

The traditional process takes an average of one week to complete, from scheduling a meeting with a JP to submitting the paper certificate to the AGD.

Payment Disruptions

Delays in submission can result in interruptions to critical pension payments, causing financial strain and stress for pensioners.

Administrative Burden

Manual verification creates a significant workload for both the AGD staff and designated notaries, leading to inefficiencies in the system.

Our Solution

eLife is a secure, optional digital pathway designed to coexist with traditional methods. It empowers pensioners who prefer or require a streamlined, remote solution to verify their life status.

  • Biometric Verification: Uses facial recognition and deepfake detection to ensure security
  • Mobile-Friendly: Accessible on smartphones for convenience
  • Digital Certificates: Automatic generation and storage of verification documents
  • Quarterly Reminders: Notifications to ensure timely submission
  • Fallback Options: Video conferencing with AGD agents when needed
